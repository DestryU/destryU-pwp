What?

The obvious question is, 'What is Interactive Design?' That's a good question, but also kind of hard to answer. If design is all about what happens when we reach out to touch the world around us, then... I guess you could say it's my job to make sure the world can reach out to touch us in meaningful and engaging ways.

Where?

Everywhere! And everything! If it was made by people and for people, then it was designed in some way. Now, not everyone is good at design - there's a lot of accidental and even plain bad design in the world. Good design is hard, complex, and takes time. Why?

That's exactly the thing - you don't notice good design. It's easy to notice bad design: fiddly handles or knobs and hard to read text. But good design? Good design is invisible. No one wants to be remembered as the product that didn't work or the website that's awful to use. Whether you're an entrepreneur thinking about your online presence or a large corporation looking to appeal to a wider audience, you need good design. And I can help with that.
