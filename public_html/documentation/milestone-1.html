Purpose

Firstly I want to use my website as an advertisement and resume for potential employers and clients, complete with clear and simple options for contacting me and finding additional information. Secondly, I want the website to serve as a lite gallery/portfolio of what I've done in the past and am currently capable of.

Target Audience

I specifically want this website to be seen and visited by potential employers, contractors, and clients. More specifically, I want people who are looking for web developers and designers that can make sleek, unique, and quality products that match their needs and at their scale. This could include large companies, small businesses, and entrepreneurial individuals that want to make sure that they are putting their best foot forward in the digital world.

Goal

My express goal for this website is to attract work and clients...

  • ... by displaying highlights of my past work
  • ... by communicating my personality through my presentation
  • ... by using the medium of the website to give an active display of my knowledge and ability
  • ... by anticipating user curiosity/interest and providing answers ahead of time

Persona

Name

Catherine Ege

Job

Catherine is the Human Relations Manager at a new startup company she believes in. Her chief responsibility is hiring new individuals that she believes will not only be a good fit for the company, but also people who can share in her excitement for what the company does.

Technologies

  • Smart Phone
  • Work Laptop
  • Personal Laptop

Attitudes

Likes

  • People
  • Outings, events, dinners, etc.

Dislikes

  • Unorganized Information
  • Repetitive work

Frustrations

  • Is frustrated by applications and resumes that are loud, noisy, and filled with fluff
  • Is frustrated by needing to dig for additional information
  • Is frustrated by hires that look good on paper, but fall short of their promises

Goals

First and foremost, Catherine wants to be able to see what a potential hire is capable of in a professional environment. This is ideally shown through past work. Secondly, Catherine wants to get a brief introduction to the personality of the person she is looking at; at the end of the day, she hires good people to get the job done. Lastly, Catherine wants to view and parse this information as quickly as possible so that she can get through as many applicants as possible in a day.
